Data Format 

Device ID| Data |SeqNumber 

Device ID :
Altimeter = 0
Compass= 1;
Gyroscope= 2

Data :

Alti: Altitude in miliimeters

Compass: Accelorometer.x , Accelorometer.y , Accelorometer.z , Magnetometer.x,
Magnetometer.y , Magnetometer.z

Gyro : X , Y , Z 

X: Vertical
Y: Perpendicular to Walking line
Z: Parallel to Walking line
